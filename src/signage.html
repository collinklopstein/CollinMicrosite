Providing complete digital signage solutions.

The Aspect Oneview wall monitor solution provides visibility into critical data, in real-time, as well as other aspect products and ensures a consistent viewing experience both in and out of the contact center.

Why it makes a difference

Aspect Oneview is flexible and scalable from design to development to deployment.

Customize Your Look and Feel

Design and branding needs are unique to each business; in addition KPIs and critical contact center metrics will vary accross Aspect business partners. Aspect Oneview provides a base set of enterprise level information, but is built using templates and modules ensuring the design, layout, and content can be tailored for each customer's business needs.

Aspect OneView is a web application pulling real time updates, based on daily contact center events and other data sources. Key contact center KPIs and general enterprise information are displayed ensuring consistent, relevant, up-to-date and engaging data across the call-center ecosystem.
